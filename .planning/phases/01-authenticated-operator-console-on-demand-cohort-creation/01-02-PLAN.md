## Phase Goal (user story)

**As a** service operator, **I want to** create and configure a cohort on demand from the console (beacon type, co-sign threshold, capacity, on my service's active network), **so that** I can prepare exactly the cohort I intend to offer, review it as an un-advertised draft, and discard it if I change my mind, without editing boot-time env vars or restarting the process.

<objective>
Deliver the create/configure/discard half of the two-step cohort flow (SVC-01, D-12/D-13) as a vertical slice on top of the Wave-1 auth foundation: gated operator routes that build and store a cohort draft app-side, validation, discard, and the operator's own cohort list - plus the web create form and list that drive them. Advertise (moving a draft to the live runner), the public directory, and loop removal are plan 03.

Purpose: An operator can shape a cohort by hand, on demand, and see it as a Draft before anything is published - the clean SVC-01/SVC-02 split that also seeds Phase 5's paused/unadvertised state.

Output: `operator-cohorts.ts` (drafts closure + create/discard/list) + its routes in `hono-adapter.ts`, wiring in `index.ts`, `operator-cohorts.spec.ts`, and the web `CreateCohortForm` + `OperatorCohortList` mounted into the console shell.
</objective>
