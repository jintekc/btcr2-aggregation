## Phase Goal (user story)

**As a** self-hosting operator (and the maintainer of this reference app), **I want to** an automated hermetic scenario that logs in, creates and advertises a cohort on demand, and drives real headless participants through join -> co-sign -> anchor -> resolve, **so that** ROADMAP success criterion 4 is proven end to end - the full lifecycle still completes for an operator-advertised cohort, driven only by the operator's action and not by any perpetual loop - and the auth boundary + loop removal cannot silently regress.

<objective>
Prove the whole Phase-1 slice works together (HOST-01 + SVC-01 + SVC-02, success criterion 4) with one hermetic tsx e2e: it authenticates as the operator, creates + advertises a cohort over the real HTTP surface, spawns headless `createParticipant` peers that join and co-sign, and confirms the cohort completes (64-byte aggregated signature) on the offline/fixture path - plus the mandatory negative auth assertions and the loop-removed boot assertion. Register it as `e2e:operator` in the existing gate.

Purpose: This is the capstone proof and a regression guard for the auth boundary and the on-demand-only driver. It closes the CONCERNS.md "auth must ship paired with its negative test" requirement and the "removed-loop / on-demand-advertise e2e" requirement in one hermetic harness.

Output: `e2e/operator-cohort.ts` and the `e2e:operator` root script.
</objective>
