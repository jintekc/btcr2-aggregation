## Phase Goal (user story)

**As a** self-hosting service operator, **I want to** sign in to a protected console and have every operator control and telemetry route enforced server-side, **so that** no unauthenticated visitor can control my service or read its operator-only telemetry, and the console is the trusted entry point for creating and advertising cohorts.

> Note: derived from the ROADMAP Phase 1 `**Goal:**` line (which is task-shaped) plus the PROJECT.md operator North Star; `/gsd mvp-phase` was not run for this pre-discussed phase.

<objective>
Ship the first authenticated, server-enforced operator surface (HOST-01) as a thin vertical slice: a Hono session guard + login/logout/session routes on the service, a fail-closed boot when `OPERATOR_PASSWORD` is unset, and a login-gated `/operator` console shell in the web SPA. This is the foundation every later slice (create, advertise, monitor, lifecycle) sits behind, so it lands in Wave 1.

Purpose: Establish that nothing mutating or operator-only is reachable without a valid session, closing CONCERNS.md's top blocker ("no auth anywhere in the control plane"). Auth ships paired with its mandatory negative tests.

Output: `operator-auth.ts` (session store + middleware + handlers), wiring in `hono-adapter.ts`/`index.ts`, fail-closed boot in `demo-server.ts`, the new `docs/adr/0015` ADR, the web `/operator` login shell, and the `operator-auth.spec.ts` + `operator-boot.spec.ts` unit tests.
</objective>
