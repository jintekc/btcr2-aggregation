## Phase Goal (user story)

**As a** participant pointed at a service's URL, **I want to** join only the specific open cohort I chose (not whatever advert happens to arrive), **so that** I take part in the cohort I actually picked and a cohort that already filled or closed cannot pull me in. (Derived from the Phase 2 ROADMAP goal + PART-02.)

<objective>
Deliver the join-by-filter mechanism (the single PART-02 change, D-14) and prove the whole browse-and-pick loop headlessly. Narrow the participant runner's `shouldJoin` from "accept every advert" to "accept only the picked cohortId", then add one fresh hermetic tsx capstone (`e2e/browse-join-cohort.ts`, modeled on `e2e/operator-cohort.ts`) that boots a real service, advertises two cohorts, and proves a participant given a picked cohortId joins ONLY that cohort, seats on `cohort-ready`, co-signs a 64-byte aggregated signature, and never seats into a non-Advertised/absent cohort.

Purpose: this is the vertical backbone of the phase - PART-02 + ROADMAP criterion 3 proven end to end over real HTTP with zero new backend/packages/routes, so the browser slices (plans 02-04) wire UI onto a mechanism already verified deterministic.

Output: `cohortId?` on `CreateParticipantOptions` + the `matchesPickedCohort` predicate in `packages/participant/src/index.ts`, its `index.spec.ts`, the `e2e/browse-join-cohort.ts` capstone, and the `e2e:browse` package script.
</objective>
