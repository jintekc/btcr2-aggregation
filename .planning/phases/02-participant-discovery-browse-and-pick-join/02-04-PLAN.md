## Phase Goal (user story)

**As a** participant who found an open cohort I want, **I want to** confirm my identity inline and join that one cohort, then see I'm seated and continue to co-sign and resolve, **so that** I take part in the cohort I chose without a separate KeyGen-first stepper and without ever losing the working lifecycle. (Derived from the Phase 2 ROADMAP goal + PART-02 + criterion 3.)

<objective>
Complete the browse-and-pick loop in the UI (PART-02, criterion 3). Add the inline identity step revealed at Join (D-03/D-04, reusing the existing KEY-generate/import store actions), give `CohortRow` its interactive Join affordance (enabled only on a joinable row, D-06 belt), and integrate `BrowseView` so a pick flows pick -> inline identity -> `store.join(baseUrl, pickedCohortId)` -> seated confirmation -> the existing submit/co-sign/resolve tail unchanged (D-11), with a Leave action and the deterministic filled/closed message from the store (D-12).

Purpose: this is the visible end of the phase - a stranger browses, picks a cohort, confirms an identity, joins by choice, is seated, and the whole shipped lifecycle still completes. It reuses plan 03's store lifecycle and plan 02's browse surface; it adds no backend, no protocol, and no new primitive.

Output: `browse/JoinIdentityStep.tsx`, the `CohortRow` Join wiring (`onPick`), and the `BrowseView` pick/identity/seated/tail/leave integration.
</objective>
