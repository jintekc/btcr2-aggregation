## Phase Goal (user story)

**As a** participant who joins a not-yet-full cohort by choice, **I want to** stay in a truthful waiting state (seeing seats fill) until the cohort fills all n seats, bounded only by the cohort's own server-side lifetime, **so that** I am never falsely told the cohort filled or closed while it is still openly Advertised. (Closes UAT gap G-02-2 under the PART-02 join-by-choice truth.)

<objective>
Close UAT gap G-02-2: the browser participant store arms a 90s join-seat grace timer (`JOIN_SEAT_GRACE_MS`, in the `cohort-joined` handler) at opt-in. Its booth-era premise (a seat is imminent after opt-in because fillers lock the cohort at threshold within seconds and the server reaps idle cohorts at 60s) was invalidated by the accepted Phase-2 gap fixes: 02-05 makes a cohort finalize only when ALL n seats fill (min == max == n), 02-06 gives an advertised cohort a 30-minute discovery window, and there are no auto-join fillers. A solo joiner now opts in, waits at `Joining...`, and at 90s the timer fires `fail('That cohort filled or closed before you were seated...')` while the cohort is still openly Advertised and joinable in the directory. `fail()` also calls `teardownLive()`, stranding the accepted opt-in as a zombie seat the protocol cannot reclaim (there is no leave signal), which can wedge the cohort in keygen when it eventually fills.

The directory-poll handler (`handleDirectorySnapshot`) already distinguishes the three states correctly (still-Advertised: keep waiting; left-Advertised while never-opted-in: true filled-or-closed; left-Advertised while opted-in: the genuinely ambiguous window). The SOLE logic defect is WHERE the grace timer arms. This plan moves the arming from `cohort-joined` (opt-in sent) to `handleDirectorySnapshot`'s opted-in-departure branch (observed departure), so the 90s window bounds only the genuine lock-to-cohort-ready ambiguity, and an opted-in participant whose picked cohort is still Advertised waits indefinitely from the client's view (bounded server-side by the cohort's own 30-min expiry, whose row-vanish the poll observes as a departure -> grace -> terminal). It also adds a truthful waiting surface: a new `awaitingSeats` store field captured from the polled row, rendered as `Waiting for the cohort to fill ({joined}/{capacity} seats)` in the join flow.

The CR-01 protection (never tear down a genuine member mid-keygen) must survive the move: the grace window still exists, it just arms at observed departure instead of at opt-in, so a real member forming with cohort-ready imminent is still protected for the full window.

Output: the arming-site move + the `awaitingSeats` field and its capture / resets + the rewritten CR-01 / wait-for-n comment block in participant.ts, a RED-first reworked participant.spec.ts, and the waiting line in JoinIdentityStep.tsx.
</objective>
