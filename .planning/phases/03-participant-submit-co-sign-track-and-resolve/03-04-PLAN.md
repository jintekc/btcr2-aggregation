<objective>
Restructure the participant store into the D-01 stage model: add the explicit-submit deferred (driving the submit window), a pure `deriveStage` render authority, an epoch-guarded anchor poll over the new public read, auto-resolve with a mode-honest three-way round-trip check, and the post-seat degraded-state detection (unreachable / cohort-ended), all ADDED around the frozen Phase-2 join-through-seat lifecycle. This is the client logic PART-03 and PART-04 both ride; the cohort page (03-05/03-06) renders it.

Purpose: One lifecycle owner gains explicit submit + tracking + resolve + honest degraded states without touching the Phase 2 join hardening.
Output: `lib/anchor.ts` client, a restructured `stores/participant.ts`, and specs for the pure predicates + teardown safety.
</objective>

## Phase Goal (user story, derived from the ROADMAP Phase 3 goal)

**As a** participant who joined a cohort by choice, **I want to** submit my DID update, take part in the cohort's n-of-n MuSig2 co-signing round, track the anchor, and resolve my updated DID, **so that** I complete the real aggregation lifecycle from the cohort I discovered, not a standalone demo stepper.

> This is the Wave 2 slice that makes the store able to drive explicit submit + tracking + resolve. It renders nothing yet; the Wave 3 cohort page (03-05) is the first user-visible slice. This ordering is the honest shape of a store-and-page rewire (the store is one file all UI routes through).
