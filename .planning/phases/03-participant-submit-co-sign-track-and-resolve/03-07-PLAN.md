<objective>
Close the two review-confirmed correctness/honesty gaps in the Phase 3 participant completion tail (03-VERIFICATION.md truths 5 and 6, elevated from 03-REVIEW.md CR-01 and WR-01). Both defects live in the participant completion path: the post-seat directory poll can false-fail a genuinely successful cohort (CR-01), and the completion summary claims a service does not broadcast when a broadcast is merely pending or failed (WR-01). Both directly undermine the phase's core promise (Success Criteria 1/2/3): a participant who submits and co-signs successfully must see that success truthfully, and a live service's anchor status must be narrated honestly (D-07 mode honesty).

Purpose: make the completion tail race-free and mode-honest so a genuine success is never reported as a failure and a broadcasting/failed live anchor is never described as a hermetic no-broadcast service.
Output: a consecutive-gone-streak guard on handlePostSeatSnapshot (SSE wins the race), a new pure anchorSummaryState selector, a shouldAutoResolve that reaches a resolve outcome on a failed broadcast, a three-way honest Signed-line branch in CompletionSummary, and unit tests pinning all of it.
</objective>
